Rangpur has become a Divisional City from a Zilla sadar. Now some days the necessity of housing & other infrastructural development is rapidly increasing. This is the time or opportunity for doing something & contributes with our best for the development of our own town to the entire citizen of Rangpur as well as for all who love our motherland. At least we can make a model city with all the modern city facilities properly as much as we can. So we think to form “RANGPUR REAL ESTATE LTD” better utilize our existing urban area to save our land for Irrigations.

Housing is no more a patterned idea rather it’s an art, senses of an aesthetic point of views while quality is never out of focus. Accompanied by devoted team of professionals with a strong financial back up the company has been working with full confidence to prove itself a new trendsetter in real estate industry.
